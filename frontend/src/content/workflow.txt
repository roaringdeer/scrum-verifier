<p>Scrum employ an iterative approach to product development. Scrum workflow runs as follows:</p>
<ol>
<li>Product Backlog creation</li>
During this meeting Product Goal is set and Product Backlog is created.
<li>Sprint</li>
This step is repeated until the product isn't finished.
<ol>
<li>Sprint Planning</li>
Setting Sprint Goal and creating Sprint Backlog
<li>Daily Scrum</li>
Daily meetings to keep everyone in team updated.
<li>Sprint Review</li>
Show the client what you managed to produce during the sprint and get the customers feedback.
<li>Sprint Retrospective</li>
Give feedback to  your team and improve the way you work.
</ol>
<li>Product delivery</li>
Finished product is released!
</ol>
